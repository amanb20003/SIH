Addition

Definition: Addition is a mathematical operation that combines two or more numbers to find their total or sum.

Examples:

Example 1: 3 + 4 = 7

Fractions

Definition: Fractions represent parts of a whole and are written as a numerator over a denominator.

Examples:

Example 1: 1/2 represents half of a whole.

Example 2: 3/4 represents three-quarters of a whole.
